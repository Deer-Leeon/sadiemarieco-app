Studio Policies

My Policies

These guidelines protect my time, your appointment, and the experience of every client I serve.

01

Cancellation Policy

I require a minimum of 48 hours' notice for any cancellations or reschedules. This allows me the opportunity to fill your appointment with another client on the waitlist.

Cancellations made with less than 48 hours' notice will forfeit their deposit and may be subject to a cancellation fee of up to 50% of the service total.
02

Late Arrivals

I kindly ask that you arrive on time. A grace period of 10 minutes is offered; however, late arrivals beyond this may result in a modified service or a need to reschedule.

Clients arriving more than 15 minutes late will be considered a no-show and charged accordingly. Please reach out before your appointment time if running behind.
03

Deposits & Payments

All appointments require a non-refundable deposit of $50 to secure your booking. This deposit is applied directly to your service total on the day of your appointment.

Remaining balances are due at the time of service. Accepted: cash, Venmo, Cash App, and all major credit cards. A 3% processing fee applies to card transactions.

By booking an appointment with Sadie Marie, you acknowledge and agree to all studio policies outlined above. Thank you for respecting my time — it means the world to me.
