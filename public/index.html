The name

Inspired by the Finnish word sade (saw-deh), meaning “rain,” Sadie Marie was created to be a space where clients can relax, recharge, and leave feeling confident and refreshed.

As a licensed cosmetologist and graduate of Taylor Andrews Academy, I’m passionate about enhancing natural beauty through personalized lash and brow services tailored to each client’s unique features and style.

My goal is to create an experience that feels both uplifting and comfortable from the moment you walk into the studio.

I can’t wait to meet you!

Studio Policies

My Policies

These guidelines protect my time, your appointment, and the experience of every client I serve.

01

Cancellation Policy

To respect both my time and yours, I kindly ask for at least 24 hours’ notice for any cancellations or rescheduling requests. Appointments canceled or rescheduled within 24 hours may incur a fee of up to 50% of the scheduled service cost.

02

No-Show Policy

No-shows will be charged 100% of the scheduled service cost. Appointments canceled or rescheduled within 2 hours of the appointment time will be considered a no-show.

03

Satisfaction Guarantee

My goal is for you to leave the studio feeling like a million bucks! If you are unsatisfied with your results, please reach out within 48 hours of your appointment so I can offer a complimentary adjustment if appropriate.

Referral Program
